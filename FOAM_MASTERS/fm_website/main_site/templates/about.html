About Foam Masters

We are a manufacturing company based in Lusaka, Zambia. Our main objective as a company is manufacture and supply world class quality furniture to our clientele.

Our speciality in the furniture business is more dominant in the manufacturing and supply of beds, matresses, sofa sets, cusions, foam sheets and foam material.
